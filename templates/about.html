About This Tool

This is an AI-powered skin disease classification tool designed for educational and screening purposes. It uses deep learning models trained on dermoscopic images to assist in identifying various types of skin lesions.

Supported Diseases

  • Melanocytic nevi (moles) - Common benign skin growths
  • Melanoma - Most serious type of skin cancer
  • Benign keratosis - Non-cancerous growths
  • Basal cell carcinoma - Most common skin cancer
  • Actinic keratoses - Precancerous lesions
  • Vascular lesions - Abnormal blood vessel formations
  • Dermatofibroma - Common non-cancerous skin nodule

Important Notice

⚠️ Disclaimer: This tool is for educational and screening purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified dermatologist for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
